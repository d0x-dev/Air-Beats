YouTube Music Integration

Material Design 3

10-Band Equalizer

Customize bass boost, treble, and genre presets with precision 10-band audio equalization DSP parameters.

Smart Offline Downloads

Download your favorite songs and albums for offline listening anytime without internet access.

Advanced Search & Discovery

Instant search across songs, albums, artists, and playlists with smart autocomplete indexing.

Ultra-sleek pitch black interface engineered specifically for OLED screen power savings during night listening.

AI-powered playlist generation that adapts to your activity, listening habits, and mood preferences.
