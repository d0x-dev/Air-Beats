Frequently Asked Questions

What is AirBeats?

AirBeats is a free, modern web music player and application offering ad-free high quality audio streaming, real-time sound visualizers, and custom audio processing.

Is AirBeats completely free to use?

Yes! AirBeats is 100% free with zero subscription fees, hidden costs, or advertisement interruptions.

Which platforms are supported by AirBeats?

AirBeats is available on Android (APK), Windows 10/11, and any modern web browser via progressive web application standard.
