Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AirBeats.

What is AirBeats? expand_more

AirBeats is a free, open-source YouTube Music client offering ad-free audio streaming, real-time visualizers, 10-band audio equalizer parameters, and offline playback.

Is AirBeats 100% free with zero ads? expand_more

Yes! AirBeats is completely free without any subscription fees, hidden paywalls, or commercial audio interruptions.

Which operating systems are supported? expand_more

AirBeats provides signed APK builds for Android (version 6.0+) and desktop executable installers (.exe) for Windows 10 & 11.
